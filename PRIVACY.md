# Privacy Policy for LBC ReLister

**Last Updated: November 2, 2025**

## Introduction

LBC ReLister ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we use it.

## Data Collection

### Error and Crash Reports

The Extension uses Sentry (sentry.io) to collect error and crash reports. This helps us identify and fix bugs to improve the Extension's reliability.

**Data collected includes:**
- Error messages and stack traces
- Extension version
- Browser type and version
- Timestamp of the error
- Context information about what action was being performed when the error occurred

**Data NOT collected:**
- Personal information (name, email, address, etc.)
- Leboncoin account credentials
- Content of your ads (titles, descriptions, prices)
- Browsing history
- Any personally identifiable information

### Data Storage and Transmission

Error data is transmitted securely via HTTPS to Sentry's servers located in the United States. Sentry processes this data in accordance with their privacy policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/

## Purpose of Data Collection

We collect error data solely to:
- Identify and fix bugs
- Improve Extension stability and performance
- Enhance user experience

## Data Retention

Error data is retained for 90 days, after which it is automatically deleted from Sentry's servers.

## Third-Party Services

The Extension uses the following third-party service:

**Sentry**: Error tracking and monitoring
- Website: https://sentry.io
- Privacy Policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your data to third parties
- Share your data with advertisers
- Use your data for marketing purposes

Error data is only shared with Sentry for the purpose of error tracking and is not shared with any other third parties.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Know what data is collected
- Request deletion of your data
- Opt out of error tracking by uninstalling the Extension

## Leboncoin Data Access

The Extension requires access to leboncoin.fr to function. However:
- All operations are performed locally in your browser
- No data from Leboncoin is sent to any third-party servers (except error logs to Sentry)
- The Extension does not store your Leboncoin credentials
- The Extension does not track your Leboncoin activity
